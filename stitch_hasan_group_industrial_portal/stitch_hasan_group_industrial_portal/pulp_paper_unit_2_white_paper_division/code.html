Unit-2: The Evolution of White Paper Engineering

01

Automated Pulp Refining

Our computer-controlled refining process ensures consistent fiber length, which is critical for the tensile strength and surface smoothness of high-grade paper.

02

Multi-Stage Coating

Double-blade coating technology applies a uniform surface layer that enhances ink absorption and prevents bleeding, making it ideal for high-speed laser printing.

03

High-Speed Calendering

Controlled pressure and temperature cycles achieve the desired gloss and smoothness level without reducing the bulk of the paper.
